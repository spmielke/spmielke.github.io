## About
<b>Steve Mielke</b> is a researcher and educator in the Austin, Texas area. He is a former programmer and analyst at NASA, NASA Postdoctoral Program fellow, NIH National Research Service Award recipient, and AAAS Mass Media fellow. 

His interdisciplinary research interests include molecular biophysics, computation, AI, complexity, history and philosophy of science, and climate change. Ongoing projects apply machine learning and other data science methodologies to a range of problems. Current focus areas include bioinformatics, biomolecular structure prediction, and neural network audio recognition.
